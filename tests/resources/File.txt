"Content" refers to the substance, information, or creative material (text, images, video) contained within a medium, book, or digital platform. It also denotes the capacity of a container, the proportion of a substance within a mixture, or the subject matter of a work. 
Key Definitions and Contexts:
Digital & Information Content: Material available on websites, social media, or in media, including articles, videos, and images.
Contained Material (Contents): Items inside a container, such as the "contents of a box".
Substance & Capacity: The amount of a specific ingredient (e.g., high-fat content) or the total volume/size something can hold.
Meaning & Subject Matter: The core message, topics, or themes in a book, speech, or artistic work.
Adjective (Content): Feeling satisfied or pleased. 
Distinctions:
Content (Noun, singular): Information or substance.
Contents (Noun, plural): Items inside a container, or a list of chapters.
Content (Adjective/Verb): Satisfied/to satisfy.
